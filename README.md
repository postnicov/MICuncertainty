# MICuncertainty

## Data and code supplementing the paper

* Postnikov E.B., Lavrova A.I. Quantifying uncertainty of tuberculosis drug susceptibility range from single-microplate test. _MethodsX_ **16** (2026) 103797 [https://doi.org/10.1016/j.mex.2026.103797]

Raw data of fluorometric measurements of the minimal inhibitory concentration of first- and second-line anti-tuberculosis drugs acting on the standard laboratory strain H37Rv of _Mycobacterium tuberculosis_  (by courtesy of the Saint-Petersburg State Research Institute of Phthisiopulmonology (Saint-Petersburg, Russia)), and the MATLAB scripts used to generate figures in the article.
